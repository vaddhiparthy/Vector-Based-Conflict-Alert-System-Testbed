# ADR-000 Template

## Title

Use a concise title (`ADR-000: <decision>`).

## Status

- Proposed / Accepted / Rejected / Superseded
- Date: YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

- Business/technical motivation.
- Constraints and assumptions.

## Decision

- What was decided and why.

## Alternatives Considered

- Alternative 1: why not selected.
- Alternative 2: why not selected.

## Consequences

- Positive impacts.
- Risks and mitigations.
- Operational impact.

## Validation

- Tests or proof points that show the decision is implemented.

## References

- Relevant specification, blog, upstream docs.
